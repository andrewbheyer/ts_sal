Detailed Description

Specifies the configuration of the durability service. That is, the service that implements the Durability.Kind of Durability.Kind#TRANSIENT and Durability.Kind#PERSISTENT.

Concerns: org.omg.dds.topic.Topic, org.omg.dds.pub.DataWriter

RxO: No

Changeable: No

This policy is used to configure the History and the ResourceLimits used by the fictitious org.omg.dds.sub.DataReader and org.omg.dds.pub.DataWriter used by the "persistence service." The "persistence service" is the one responsible for implementing Durability.Kind#TRANSIENT and Durability.Kind#PERSISTENT.
