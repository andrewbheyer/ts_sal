Stream camera.TC.zone2

Description

This subsystem comprises the devices and controllers necessary for thermal monitoring and control of the Back End Electronics attached to the bottom plate of the Inner Cryostat. The temperature will be in the neighborhood of -20C but does not require high accuracy or stability. Its function is to remove heat generated by the Back End Electronics.

Update frequency

This telemetry stream publishes a new record at 1 Hz

Publishers

Only one instance of this stream is published.
