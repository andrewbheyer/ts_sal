Document Title

                                          
 

Camera Telemetry List

                                         
                                            
 

Purpose

                                          
  

This document itemizes the telemetry that is generated by the LSST camera.  It lists the location (physical and subsystem name), type, channel count, and nightly volume of each telemetry item, and the name of the entry in the observatory’s facility database.
